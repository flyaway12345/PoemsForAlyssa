Alyssa Effects End

The feelings I feel whether real or false
Make my heart skip a beat with every other pulse
Although many other men perseverate on the Alyssa effect
Her humor of mind and contents of soul are what lead me by the neck

Miss Dawn's Mother

They say that a cat's personality is indicative of their owner
In Little Miss Dawn is quite the show-er
Silly and kind, passionate with pride
All of the traits that keeps her mother in my mind

A Thought From RDO

The west is vast
And the campfire crackles on
No matter how long the journey
The thought of her drives me on

Moments

Kindness abound and a depth of care
A large warm smile and gorgeous blue hair
When you speak to me my heart springs a flutter
I love nothing more then moments spent with one another

Small Meetings

Hair like the sea and a smile so warm
Your presence brings joy I rarely felt before
Times are complex and feelings are sometimes fleeting
But all the time I spend with you has been worth every single meeting

Review

Everything is different and the future is new
The one thing I've found as i build everything back
